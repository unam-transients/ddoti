## Alignment

Checked C1 in image 20260526T041124C1o.fits.fz. FWHM is about 2 pixels along a diagonal from UL to BR and is about 4 pixels in UR and LL.

Using 1.27 mm hex key for the lock screws and 10 mm wrench for the adjustment screws.
